Core toolkit

Purpose-built around the auction workflow.

Each feature supports a specific part of the auction journey, from preparing the listing to converting the winning bid into a completed sale.

Timed online auctions

Run clear bidding windows with reserve visibility, final countdown states, and a transparent audit trail for every bid.

Buyer registration

Capture bidder details, verification status, deposit readiness, and enquiry notes before buyers can place bids.

Legal pack management

Upload, organise, and monitor access to legal documents so serious buyers have the information they need before bidding.

Portal-ready publishing

Prepare consistent listing data and keep connected channels aligned as auction details change.

Auction dashboard

See active auctions, bidder activity, document views, reserve status, and next actions without chasing spreadsheets.

Live bid monitoring

Track bids as they happen, surface bidder confidence signals, and keep vendors informed with accurate status updates.

Automated communications

Send outbid alerts, auction reminders, viewing updates, and winning bidder instructions without manual follow-up.

Compliance support
