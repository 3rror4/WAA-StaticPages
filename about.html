About We Are Auctions

We help estate agents make auctions a serious sales channel.

Our platform is built for agencies that want the speed of auction selling without losing control of their brand, process, or fee structure.

Our mission

Modern auction tools for modern agency teams.

Traditional property sales can leave vendors waiting, teams chasing updates, and buyers unclear on next steps. We Are Auctions exists to give estate agents a practical way to add urgency, transparency, and structure to property sales.

We focus on the operational details that matter: clear auction setup, qualified bidders, organised legal documents, connected listing workflows, and a record of every meaningful action.

  • Built around the needs of estate agents, not generic marketplace sellers
  • Designed to support branch workflows and agency branding
  • Focused on transparent pricing and fee control
Why we built it

Estate agents needed a better auction operating model.

The market already understands auctions. The challenge is giving branch teams a professional system for running them well.

Traditional sales can drag

Auction deadlines help focus vendors and buyers around a defined process, making momentum easier to maintain.

Systems are often fragmented

Spreadsheets, portals, emails, documents, and bidder updates should not all live in separate places.

Fee models need clarity

Agencies should be able to use auction technology without giving up control over how they charge and earn.

Values

How we make product decisions.

Operational clarity

Every feature should make it easier for a branch team to know what is happening, what has changed, and what needs attention.

Commercial transparency

Pricing, fees, and responsibilities should be clear for agencies, vendors, and buyers throughout the auction journey.

Practical quality

We prioritise reliable workflows, useful reporting, and interfaces that working estate agents can use every day.
