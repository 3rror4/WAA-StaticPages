FAQ

Pricing questions estate agents ask first.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Agencies can start with a 14-day trial of the Professional plan so your team can explore the workflow before committing.

Do you take a cut of my commission?

No. The platform pricing is separate from your vendor and buyer fee structure. Your agency remains in control of its own commercial model.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. You can move up or down as auction volume changes. Larger agencies can move to an Enterprise setup when custom integrations or permissions are needed.

What payment methods are supported?

We support major cards and direct debit for UK accounts. Enterprise customers can discuss invoice billing during onboarding.

Do you provide onboarding?

Yes. Professional and Enterprise plans include guided onboarding so your team can launch its first auctions with the correct workflow in place.

Need help choosing the right plan?

Tell us how many auctions you expect to run and which systems your branch uses today. We will recommend the practical starting point.
